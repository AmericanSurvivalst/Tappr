Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you use Tappr, we collect:

  • Account information (email, name) when you sign up
  • Profile information you choose to share (contact details, links, photo)
  • Usage data (profile views, tap analytics)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve Tappr services
  • Display your profile when someone taps your card
  • Show you analytics about your profile views
  • Send important account notifications
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions

Information Sharing

We don't sell your data. We only share information:

  • When you choose to share it (your public profile)
  • With service providers who help run Tappr (hosting, payments)
  • If required by law

Your Profile Visibility

Your Tappr profile is public by design—that's how people can see your contact info when they tap your card. You control exactly what information appears on your profile.

Data Security

We use encryption and secure infrastructure to protect your data. Payment information is handled by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your profile anytime
  • Export your data
  • Close your account

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and analytics cookies to understand how people use Tappr. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Questions? Email us at hello@tappr.one
