Questions?

Everything you need to know about Tappr.

How does Tappr work?+

Your Tappr card has an NFC chip inside. When you tap it against someone's phone, it opens your profile in their browser instantly. No app needed on either end.

Do I need to install an app?+

Nope. The person you're sharing with doesn't need an app either. Your profile opens directly in their web browser.

What if I change jobs or phone numbers?+

Just update your profile at app.tappr.one. Changes appear instantly. No reprinting, no new card needed.

Which phones work with NFC?+

All iPhones from XR/XS and newer, plus most Android phones from the last 5 years. That covers about 90% of smartphones.

What's included with the free card?+

The plastic card is included free with any subscription. Wood cards are $10 with subscription (50% off), metal cards are $15 with subscription (50% off).

How is this different from Popl or Linq?+

Way cheaper. Popl charges $90-180 for the first year. Tappr is $25-36/year for premium with a card included. And our basic cards work forever even without a subscription.
